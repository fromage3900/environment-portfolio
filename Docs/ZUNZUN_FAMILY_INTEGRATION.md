Each of the **seven travelers** is unconsciously guarding one of the seven directional gateways to the Astral Threshold ΓÇö the boundary between worlds that **Melusina** protects.

- **Zundamon** guards the East gate ΓÇö but she didn't know it. Her Zunda Arrow accidentally pierced the gate, letting her cross into Melodia's realm.
- **Zunko, Kiritan, and Itako** are the Tohoku sisters who came looking for Zundamon. They discovered their own gate-guardian nature in the process.
- **Metan, Sora, and Tsurugi** arrived from their respective regions, each drawn by the resonance of Melusina's celestial energy bleeding through the Threshold.

Their convergence is NOT coincidence. Melusina's power is fading (the central conflict of the game), and the Threshold is cracking. The seven guardians are being pulled toward the center of the cosmos by an ancient pact ΓÇö if Melusina falls, one of them must take her place.

**The game's true ending depends on which guardian the player has bonded with most.**
